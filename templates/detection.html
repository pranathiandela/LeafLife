Tips for better results

  • Upload clear, well-lit leaf images
  • Avoid blurry or low-light images
  • Capture both sides of the leaf if possible
  • Focus on the most affected area of the leaf

For the most accurate AI diagnosis, photograph the leaf the same way our training images were captured — isolated against a plain background, not while still attached to the plant.
